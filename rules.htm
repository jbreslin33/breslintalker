We will be playing a scramble as always:

1: Take the best shot of your 3 or 4 teammates
2: No minimum or maximun on how many shots a player can use

IMPORTANT: You may move your ball one club length.
--ball must not be moved CLOSER to the hole --ball cannot be move to different type of lie.
(I.E. if your shot lands in bunker you must play from bunker, if shot
lands in ruff you must play from ruff. If ball landed on fringe you must play from fringe.
Common example of this rule: The ball you choose to play lands in the ruff along the tree line off to the right.
You may move the ball to the left one club length but only if it doesn't take you into the fairway! Cabish!
